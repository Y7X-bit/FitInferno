## 🧠 What is FitInferno?

**FitInferno** is your intelligent personal trainer.  
Just enter your:
- ✅ Weight (kg)
- ✅ Height (cm)
- ✅ Fitness goal (💪 Lose Weight, 🏋️ Gain Muscle, 🧘 Stay Fit)

…and FitInferno will instantly generate:
- 📊 BMI & health status
- 🏋️ Custom workout plan
- 🥗 Smart diet tips
